Hello, I'm

GeoSpatial Analyst / Ecological Conservation

As a graduate of Clark University with a Master's degree in Geographic Information Science, my expertise lies in applying geospatial advanced analytics, particularly in ecological conservation. My education and experience have equipped me with the skills to use these technologies to effectively mitigate the environmental impacts of human activities and aid in the recovery of natural ecosystems. This pursuit has been a long-standing ambition of mine, reflecting a deep-seated commitment to environmental stewardship and sustainable development.

My past research was mainly on the distribution survey of wildlife. These fieldwork experiences have laid the foundation for my knowledge of ecological conservation.
