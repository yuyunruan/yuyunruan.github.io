GeoSpatial Analyst / Ecological Conservation

I am a graduate student majoring in Geographic Information Science at Clark University. I'm learning to use various geospatial advanced analytics, especially in ecological conservation. I hope to utilize these technologies to mitigate the environmental impact of human activity or to help the natural ecosystem recover. For a long time, this has been my life's ambition.

My past research was mainly on the distribution survey of wildlife. These fieldwork experiences have laid the foundation for my knowledge of ecological conservation.
